# Foundations in AI Engineering

**A hands-on workshop for developers beginning their AI adoption journey**

---

## What You'll Learn

Transform your development workflow with AI-powered tools and techniques. This 105-minute workshop covers everything you need to start building with AI today.

### Key Takeaways
- Understand what AI engineering is and how it fits into modern software development
- Compare and evaluate AI tools (Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot) for your use cases
- Master prompt engineering techniques that get better results
- Configure agent memory systems for consistent, context-aware interactions
- Integrate AI tools into your existing development workflow

---

## Workshop Agenda

| Time | Topic | What You'll Do |
|------|-------|----------------|
| 10 min | **Introduction** | Understand why AI adoption is accelerating now |
| 15 min | **What is AI Engineering** | Define the role, skills, and mindset needed |
| 15 min | **AI Tools Landscape** | Compare tool categories and key players |
| 15 min | **Working with LLMs** | Learn about context windows, tokens, and prompting basics |
| 20 min | **Prompt Engineering** | Practice proven techniques and patterns |
| 15 min | **Agent Memory** | Configure AI agents with context files (CLAUDE.md, etc.) |
| 5 min | **Practical Applications** | See real use cases across the development lifecycle |
| 10 min | **Getting Started & Q&A** | Get your next steps and discuss with peers |

**Total Duration:** 1 hour 45 minutes
