# The AI Engineer Role

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-8 mt-6">

<div>

### What AI Engineers Do

- Design AI-augmented systems
- Craft effective prompts and workflows
- Integrate LLMs into applications
- Build agents and automation
- Evaluate and improve AI outputs
- Manage context and memory

</div>

<div>

### It's NOT About

- <carbon-close class="text-red-400" /> Replacing human judgment
- <carbon-close class="text-red-400" /> Building LLMs from scratch
- <carbon-close class="text-red-400" /> Knowing all the math
- <carbon-close class="text-red-400" /> Abandoning traditional skills

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-4 bg-yellow-500/10 rounded-lg">

**Key Insight:** AI Engineering sits at the intersection of software engineering, product thinking, and understanding how language models work.

</div>
